Seolleongtang

Description

Seolleongtang is a nutritious ox bone broth usually enjoyed during cold winters, either as a soup base or on its own.

Ingredients

  1. 3-4 Lbs Crosscut beef bones
  2. 1-2 Lbs Beef high in connective tissue (Brisket or Shank)

Instructions

  1. Soak bones in cold water to purge blood, about 1 hour. Do the same with the meat.
  2. Add bones to large stockpot. Add enough water to cover and bring to boil for five minutes.
  3. Drain and rinse bones of scum. Clean pot and add bones again.
  4. Fill pot with enough cold water to cover bones, bring to boil over high heat and reduce to light boil.
  5. Boil until broth becomes rich and creamy, at least 5 hours.
  6. Top off with water as needed.
  7. Add soaked meat and simmer until tender.
  8. Optionally, skim the fat and scum for a more refined flavor.
  9. Enjoy warm or freeze for later.
